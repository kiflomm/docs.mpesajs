# Auth Page

Welcome to the authentication page of our application. Here, you can find information on how to log in, register, and manage your account.

## Login

To log in, please enter your credentials below:

- **Email:** Your registered email address.
- **Password:** Your account password.

Make sure to keep your password secure and do not share it with anyone.

## Registration

If you do not have an account, you can register by providing the following information:

- **Name:** Your full name.
- **Email:** A valid email address.
- **Password:** Choose a strong password.

After registration, you will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

## Password Recovery

If you have forgotten your password, you can recover it by clicking on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Follow the instructions in the email you receive to reset your password.

## Additional Resources

For more information, please refer to our [documentation](https://example.com/docs) or contact support.

We hope you have a great experience using our application!
